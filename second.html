5. Eurasian Eagle-Owl

The eurasian eagle owl resides in Eurasia. They are far from distinct, and on the conservation status rank as "least concern", being one of the most prominent species of owl found in their respective habitats. With the female eagle-owl's wingspan being over 180cm in length when extended in flight, the eurasian eagle-owl is among one of the largest flying avians.
