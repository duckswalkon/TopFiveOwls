2. Tawny Owl

The tawny owl, commonly known as the brown owl, is found in the woodlands of throughout Europe, Eurasia and Siberia. Formidable and resilient, the tawny owl is able to withstand harsher elements than its cousins. A robust bird, the tawny owl's intermediate size allows it to both use its dimensions to scare competitors from both the same and different species, but also allows it to enter smaller crevices to pursue its pray.
