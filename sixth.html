1. Lapland Owl

More commonly known as the great grey owl, the lapland owl indeed is great in size and its ability to hunt prey unmatched. Like every owl, the lapland owl uses its strong, hooked and sharp talons to grab onto prey and crush their bones without much issue. Among one of the larger owls, the lapland owl. With little to no taxonomical differences inter-continentally, the lapland owl can be found both in northern Europe and North America. As a species, it is recognized as "the great grey" in the United States, but its prominence in areas such as Northern Finland has earned it its title as THE lapland owl. Living among other owls in Lapland, this owl is named after the place, attributing to its mighty status as a fierce predator.
