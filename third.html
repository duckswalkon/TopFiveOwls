4. Pygmy Owl

Whereas the pygmy owl is dwarfed by other subspecies of owl in size, it is not beaten in pure cuteness. Most commonly found in North America and Eurasia, the pygmy owl sports a colorful tinged feather coat, ranging from vanilla red to dark grey. While the pygmy owls cannot be properly domesticated, they remain as one of the more adorable winged beasts.
